REVERSE (Rapid Evaluation of Experimental RNA Selection/Evolution) is a webserver designed to rapidly identify, cluster, and
analyze nucleic acid sequences from large sequencing files to generate usable results with a few clicks.

What is REVERSE?

REVERSE takes as input multiple FASTQ files from high-throughput sequencing (HTS) data obtained from different rounds of in vitro selection/evolution experiments and branches into various pipelines depending on the user's analysis goals.
