Demo Files

The demo zip file contains 4 rounds from a previous in vitro selection. We will explore the capabilities of REVERSE in the tutorial by using these data.



Download Demo Files

Walkthrough

1) Download the zip file containing Demo Files above.

2) Move the downloaded file to your Desktop and double-click to unzip the file.

3) From the REVERSE homepage, click Analyze in the navigation bar.

4) For STEP 1, enter '4' rounds, for STEP 2, enter '99' for the quality cutoff.

4) Under Upload Round 1, select Choose File and navigate to the folder you downloaded. Click on the file called 'round1_demo.zip'. Repeat uploading the files for rounds 2-4 and then click Upload.

5) On the following screen, don't worry right now about downloading the high quality sequences. Skip to STEP 3a. Enter '33' in the first box and '72' in the second box (this is the region of interest for this experiment). For STEP 3b, click 'correct'. For STEP 3c, click 'Cluster Sequences and Analyze Sequence Clusters'.

6) We are going to use 2 of the many tools in REVERSE. First scroll down to Track Clusters Through Selection, which identifies the peak sequence of each cluster and finds its abundance in all previous rounds. In both of the input boxes, enter '10' and click Analyze. This shows you the emergence dynamics of the most abundant sequence clusters in the selection.

7) Press the back arrow in your browser and then scroll down to Conserved Positions. Enter '90', '10', '1', 'reds' for the required parameters and click Analyze. This calculates the degree of conservation at each nucleotide position in the sequences from the most abundant sequence cluster.

Now, you can explore the other tools in REVERSE. ENJOY!
