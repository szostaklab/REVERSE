How does REVERSE work?

REVERSE is a web served platform to analyze high-throughput sequencing data from in vitro selection/evolution data. Through custom python codes run on uploaded FASTQ files, REVERSE can perform a multitude of functions. Most of these center around identifying 'important' sequences from your selection/evolution experiment and then going through each of the rounds to determine the dynamics of those top sequences.
